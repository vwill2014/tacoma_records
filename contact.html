Tacoma Records, LLC., has locations in Washington State and California. Our 1200-foot recording studio is located in downtown Tacoma in the Court House Square. Specialized facility for sound recording, mixing and audio production of instrumental or vocal musical performances, spoken words and other sounds. They range in size from a small in-home “project studio” large enough to record a single singer-guitarist, to a large building with space for a full orchestra of 20 or more musicians.
