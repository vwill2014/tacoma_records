OUR LABEL

Tacoma Records is an American record label founded by two cousins, Quinton Davis and John Penton, in Tacoma, WA, in 2017. Tacoma Records specializes in Pop, R & B, Soul, and Gospel Music. After several trips to Los Angeles, Davis and Penton collaborated with industry heads with Universal Music Group, and formed a plan to discover Tacoma-Seattle artists and introduce them to the industry. Tacoma Records runs two recording studios with top producers, a writing team and instrumentalists (drums, percussion, brass, woodwind),which have over 20 years of recorded music history. Tacoma Records is a premiere label with connections to industry greats who are excited about tapping into the Pacific Northwest.

Our recording studios may be used to record singers, instrumental musicians (e.g., electric guitar, piano, saxophone, or ensembles such as orchestras), voice-over artists for advertisements or dialogue replacement in film, television or animation, or to record accompanying musical soundtracks.
